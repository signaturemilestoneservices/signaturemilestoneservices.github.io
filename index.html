About Me

I didn't take a straight path — and I wouldn't change that.

My career started in a kindergarten classroom, where I learned patience, structure, and what it means to be someone people can count on. I carried that into over a decade with Xerox in customer service and training, sharpening the people skills that have shaped everything I've done since.

From there, I moved into hospitality and event planning across hotels, private catering, and an upscale private club. The heart of it all has been over 20 years serving the military community as an event manager at Joint Base Andrews, planning and executing events at the highest levels, with care and precision. And through it all, weddings have always been my favorite.

Now I'm focused on work that feels personal — helping people through meaningful moments and making the process simple, comfortable, and stress-free. Many of the people I've worked with have become more than clients. They've become friends. And to me, that says everything.
